About Xiongji Steel

Your trusted partner in high-quality steel solutions since 2002. Learn about our journey, values, and commitment to excellence.

Our Journey

Established in 2002, Shanghai Xiongji Material Co., Ltd. started as a small steel trading company in Minhang District, Shanghai. Over the years, we've grown into an international private enterprise integrating domestic and international steel trade, processing and distribution, warehousing logistics, industry and investment.

Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has allowed us to expand our operations and build strong relationships with clients and partners around the world.

Today, we're proud to be a trusted name in the steel industry, providing premium products to construction, manufacturing, and infrastructure sectors globally.
