Galvanized Steel Coil

Galvanized Steel Coil is a high-quality steel product coated with a layer of zinc to provide superior corrosion resistance. It is widely used in construction, automotive, and home appliance industries due to its durability and versatility.

Our galvanized steel coils are available in various thicknesses, widths, and zinc coating weights to meet the specific requirements of our customers. We ensure that our products comply with international standards such as JIS G3302, ASTM A653, and EN 10346.

Specifications

Galvanized Steel Coil

High-quality hot-dipped galvanized steel coils with various zinc coating weights, suitable for construction, automotive, and home appliance applications.

Specifications:

  • Standard: JIS G3302, ASTM A653, EN 10346
  • Thickness: 0.12-3.0mm
  • Width: 600-1500mm
  • Zinc Coating: Z60-Z600 g/m²

Key Advantages

  • Superior corrosion resistance extending product lifespan
  • Cost-effective protection compared to other coatings
  • Excellent scratch and abrasion resistance
  • Versatile for both indoor and outdoor applications
  • Multiple coating thickness options (Z60-Z600)
  • 100% recyclable, environmentally friendly material
