About Xiongji Steel

We are a leading manufacturer and exporter of steel products with over 20 years of experience in the industry.

Our Story

Established in 2000, Xiongji Steel has grown from a small local supplier to a major international exporter of high-quality steel products. We specialize in manufacturing a wide range of steel products including H-beams, I-beams, steel pipes, steel coils, and more.

With our state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities, skilled workforce, and commitment to quality, we have built a strong reputation for delivering products that meet international standards and satisfy customer requirements.

Our Values

Quality

We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of quality in all our products. Our rigorous quality control processes ensure that every product that leaves our facility meets international standards.

Integrity

We believe in conducting business with honesty, transparency, and ethical practices. We build long-term relationships with our customers based on trust and mutual respect.

Innovation

We continuously strive to improve our products and processes through innovation and technology. We invest in research and development to stay ahead of market trends and meet evolving customer needs.
